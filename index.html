Why Choose SFA

Signs For All envisions a world where communication between people with hearing impairment and normal people is bridged through teaching sign language lessons to community at utmost zero cost! It is delivered by use of well qualified, trained and competent sign language instructors.

  • 01Teaching Aids

    We have well compiled researched up to date signs and curriculum to be delivered. The mode of delivery depends on the organisation of participants but mostly taught in groups of 10-15 on wards.

  • 02 Learning Aids

    We have a variety of learning aids like dictionaries, online apps, and charts that participants are able to use comfartably and learn signs.

  • 03 Partnership

    To create any desirous change in society, it starts with me and you. I believe if you partner with us, you will enable us to reach the target goal and aim. This project is free of charge and it is through partnership that we are able to teach the community free of charge.

 

core values

Vision

A world where there are no barriers to communication and full participation of persons with hearing impairement to access any forms of service without discrimination.

Mission

To enhance and promote active participation of people with hearing impairment to access services and equal opportunity that brings inclusion through teaching of sign language.
